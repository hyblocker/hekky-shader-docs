![Shader Inspector](/en/whoasked_shader_inspector_full.webp)

## Rings

- **Rings** Number of rings to render.
- **Ring Color** Color of the rings.
- **Ring Thickness** Thickness of each ring.
- **Ring Emission** How emissive the ring should be.

## Radar

- **Rings** Number of rings to render.
- **Ring Color** Color of the rings.
- **Ring Thickness** Thickness of each ring.
- **Ring Emission** How emissive the ring should be.

## AudioLink

- **Rings** Number of rings to render.
